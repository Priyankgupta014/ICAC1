    Guidelines

  • ICAC-2024 invites faculty, researchers, industrial participants and students to submit their original research work in Advances in computing
  • Authors are requested to submit their original papers in IEEE format (not published or presented earlier) in Word format only through easy chair conference link given below
  • Easy ChairPaper Submission Link
  • The corresponding author must certify that all the data presented in his/her paper has been acquired in keeping with recognized ethical research practices and plagiarism free.
  • The paper acceptance will be send to the corresponding authors for presentation only.
  • For International delegates only The Paper presentation will be Virtual (Through google meet)

    Important Note

  • Few selected papers will be published in web of Science/ SCOPUS/ UGC care list/ peer reviewed journals after the conference as per the journal guidelines
  • Authors of the selected paper will be requested for additional fee payment as per the journal guidelines (if applicable)
